About Us

The mission of Erbadiyya Quran Academy is to make Quran learning simple, effective and easier. With simple learning modules and teachers with par excellent track record, the academy envisions to make sure the children of modern generation get in touch with the Holy Quran throughout their life. Our Quran theatre with advanced technology and sound systems helps the students to memorize quickly and recite in beautiful styles. Here the concept of memorizing is simplified with the help of scientific techniques and practices.

Our Academy is located in Peringad village, a beautiful hilly area, in Kerala state. It is run by Peringad Muslim Jema’ah & Charitable trust. Erbadiyya Quran Academy is not just like any other Hifz Quran tutoring center, but a full-fledged Quran learning Institute having the best practices and facilities.

Quran is the ultimate source of guidance for mankind. It has been guiding since 1400 years after revealed to the Prophet of Islam – Mohammed PBUH. Learning Quran can help human beings on the right track, the path of righteousness and thus gain success both in this world and hereafter. We at Erbadiyya Quran Academy offer the most effective ways of learning and memorizing the words of Allah .


Erbadiyya Quran Academy aspires to be the ultimate Quran learning platform for our children. It aspires to reach to every hungry souls willing to seek a place to learn and memorize Quran. Our teachers and trainers are selected for excellence in their character and Quran teaching

Courses

Specially Curated Courses for building an Islamic Lifestyle

Memorizing Quran

This is a course for students who want to memorize the Quran. This course is not restricted to a number of months and completely depends upon the student’s capacity to learn and memorize it. The concept of memorizing is simplified with the help of scientific techniques and technology . Under the training of expert teachers your children can easily learn and memorize the Quran with greater accuracy and Tajweed rules. They can practice the reciting styles of well known Quran reciters in the our Quran theatre using advanced sound devices. You can contact us to enroll with us with this program.

Learning Quran with Tajweed and Thafseer

The students are taught to learn and read the Quran with Tajweed and its interpretation.. The six months course is a comprehensive program for the students to learn and master the Quran with Tajweed and basic explanations. Enroll with us the six months course to learn to read and understand the words of Allah.

Special Course for Kids

This course from Erbadiyya Quran Academy is designed exclusively for your small kids to help them read the Quran with correct pronunciation. It comprises of the basic Arabic alphabets with some interesting stories that give the glimpse of the Holy Quran and Islamic History in brief. Your kids in the age group of 3-4 years can join this program to get the crux of the Quran and Islam. The best qualified and experienced teachers train your kids by adopting creative methods to make the learning effective.

Qirat course

It is a specially designed course for common people who want to recite the Quran with proper Arabic accent and pronunciation. At Quran Academy, you get the opportunity to learn the art of Qirat from our experienced and qualified teachers on a one-on-one basis. The learners can schedule the learning time according to their convenience.
